What types of Yoga do we teach?

We practice 3 types of Yoga and we cater for all all age groups and skill levels.

Yin Yoga

  • Slow paced style of yoga as exercise.
  • incorporates principles of traditional Chinese medicine.
  • Postures are held for longer than in other styles.
  • Advanced practitioners stay in a posture for 5 minutes or more.

Hatha-Yoga

  • Hatha is considered an umbrella term for many types of yoga.
  • You move your body slowly and deliberately into different poses.
  • Challenge your strength and flexibility.
  • While focusing on relaxation and mindfulness.

Gentle Yoga / Meditation

  • Gentler style of Hatha yoga.
  • Performed at slower pace.
  • Extended time for meditation, breathwork and relaxation.
