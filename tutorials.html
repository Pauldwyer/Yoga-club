Basic Yoga Positions for Begineers

Mountain Pose

This two-footed stance is the foundation for many other positions that require awareness and balance. It is through this pose that one finds the proper alignment and shape for additional movements

Stand with feet together and arms at your side. Ground your feet, straighten your legs, tuck your tailbone in. As you inhale elongate through your torso and extend your arms up. Exhale and release your shoulder blades away from your head, toward the back of your waist as you release arms back to your sides.
